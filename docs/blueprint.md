# **App Name**: Páscoa Lucrativa Essencial

## Core Features:

- Course Content Delivery: Secure access for authenticated users to video lessons, text modules, and supporting materials covering product ideas, pricing strategies, and production methods.
- User Account & Authentication: Allow users to register, log in, manage their profile, and securely access their purchased course content.
- Payment Gateway Integration: Enable secure processing of course purchases with major payment providers, offering an immediate access experience.
- AI Pricing Strategy Tool: An interactive tool that uses AI to recommend optimal selling prices for confectionery products based on user input for ingredient costs, desired profit margins, and production time, explaining the underlying rationale.
- Downloadable Resources Access: A dedicated section for users to download practical assets like automated pricing spreadsheets, production checklists, Easter timelines, and supplier lists.
- Lesson Progress Tracking: Users can mark individual lessons as complete and view their overall progress through the course content.
- Dynamic Landing Page Sections: Display compelling content including problem/solution framing, benefits, testimonials, authority presentation, bonuses, urgency messages, and an FAQ to guide potential customers towards enrollment.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme.
- Primary color: Muted rose (#AD8592), offering a sophisticated and warm neutrality that suggests confectionery and refined strategy.
- Background color: Very light, warm off-white (#F4F1F2), providing a clean and inviting canvas for content.
- Accent color: Vibrant red-orange (#DC563C), used sparingly for calls-to-action and key highlights to draw attention and evoke profitability.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' (humanist sans-serif) for its elegant and artistic feel, reflecting the craftsmanship of confectionery.
- Body font: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif) to provide excellent readability for longer text blocks, with a contemporary and intellectual touch for educational content.
- Use clean, modern, and inviting icons, especially for checkmarks and feature highlights, to maintain a professional yet approachable aesthetic.
- Responsive layout with clear content hierarchy, optimizing for both engaging hero sections and easy-to-read educational content, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.
- Subtle and graceful hover effects and transitions, particularly on calls-to-action and interactive elements, to enhance user engagement without distraction.
